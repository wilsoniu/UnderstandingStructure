In this screen you will see the music list recommendation from apple. It will be updated each week and all the playlists are displayed horizontally.

There is also a recently played section.

 

 

The upper right is the profile of you, you can click it to view or edit your personal info, you can also tap show more to see more music history.

 

 

You can see today’s date on the very top of this screen.

In the upper middle is the for you playlist which were generated by apple, you can tap it and view the list.

 

There is also a recently played section where you can see the albums you have played recently.

The bottom is the same as the Library.

 

 

The title For You is very obvious, under that the playlists are very interesting because of their vibrant color. After that the recently played albums will be the next thing to see.
