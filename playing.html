This Screen is the music player, showing the music you are listening right now.

You can see the album cover art in a larger format. You can simply choose the playing time and switch songs tapping the large buttons.

 

There is an airplay button where you can tap and choose a device to stream your music to if you have one.

The three little dots is the more menu, where you can find more options about the music.

 

 

The timeline allows you to choose the time of the playing music. Under that is the Music Title and the album name followed by the artist name.

The last, pause and next button are displayed with the simple icons.

You can adjust volume using the volume slider below.

The pause button and the last next button will be the second thing people notice. The song name is also clear and displayed in bold.

The time slider and volume slider will be the next thing to notice.

The album cover is the most noticeable feature because it takes a larger area and always likely to be an interesting image.
