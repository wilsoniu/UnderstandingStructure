This screen is the music library, where all my music are saved, music can be added from Apple Music discover page, by searching or be uploaded via desktop. The newest album I ever added will be displayed on the top. Here is where I usually start.

The bottom section is a mini player where I can quickly skip to the next song or pause, if I tap this section, it will open up the full screen music player.

 

The mini player in the bottom allows people to switch or pause music quickly.

Finally the bottom menu can be reached easily since and switch to other pages.

 

Each music or album I have added before are shown by their square album cover image, once I click the image I can view the album and play the song.

 

Every album cover is grabbing people’s attention, people can see and chose the song the want to play and it also allows artists to express their music with visual language. Album Names and Artists are also displayed under each album cover, allow people to understand easily
